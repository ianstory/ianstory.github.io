What is SpaceX?

According to their website,SpaceX designs, manufactures, and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft. The company was founded in 2002 to revolutionize space technology, with the ultimate goal of enabling people to live on other planets. - From the SpaceX about page

SpaceX was founded by PayPal entrepreneur and founder of Tesla motors Elon Musk in 2002, and has since shown increasing activity in contruction and launching of their spacecraft. So far they currently have three vehicles:

The Falcon 9
A two-stage rocket designed to transport satellites and the Dragon.
The Falcon Heavy
A super-heavy lift space launch vehicle that will be the most powerful rocket in the world when it launches in 2017.
The Dragon
A spacecraft that is free-flying and designed to carry both cargo and people.

SpaceX have also produced many impressive achievements since their start, including:

  • Consructing the first private liquid-fueled rocket
  • The first private company to launch and recover a spacecraft
  • The first privately owned company to send their own spacececraft to the international space station
  • The first private company to send a satellite into space with an orbit in sync with the Earth
  • The first private company to reland a rocket capable of achieving orbit

Current Ambitions

SpaceX is currently working on meeting one of their primary goals: making life interplanetary. Specifically, they plan on sending people to colonize Mars. They are working on what they are calling an Interplanetary Transport Sysytem. This system is projected to be able to carry roughly 450 tons of cargo to Mars, with the rocket detaching and returning to Earth for reuse. SpaceX is planning to launch the first uncrewed system in 2018.
